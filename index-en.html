Biodiversica is an ongoing initiative created by Kau√™ Werner in 2025, with the goal of strengthening biodiversity conservation efforts in Brazil through audio technologies. It focuses on the development, support, and dissemination of free software and open source solutions for the collection, monitoring, management, and analysis of acoustic data from the country's different biomes.

The main focus is to provide technical support to institutions and organizations working in research, environmental monitoring, and conservation. This includes everything from firmware development for remote monitoring devices to the creation and/or training of custom computational models for data analysis.
